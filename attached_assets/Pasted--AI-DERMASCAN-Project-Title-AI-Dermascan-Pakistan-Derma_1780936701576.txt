# AI DERMASCAN 
## Project Title

**AI Dermascan – Pakistan Dermatology Initiative**

Build a production-grade, full-stack AI-powered skin cancer detection and dermatology management platform with advanced lesion segmentation, heatmap visualization, patient management, doctor consultation, medical reporting, and real-time analytics.

The system should have a premium medical dark theme, professional hospital-grade dashboard, responsive UI, and modern SaaS architecture.

---

# PROJECT OVERVIEW

AI Dermascan is an intelligent healthcare platform designed for:

* Patients
* Dermatologists
* Hospitals
* Clinics
* Medical Researchers

The platform allows users to:

* Upload skin lesion images
* Perform AI-powered skin cancer detection
* Generate lesion segmentation masks
* Visualize AI heatmaps
* Calculate confidence scores
* Assess risk levels
* Generate PDF reports
* Track patient history
* Consult dermatologists
* Book appointments
* Receive notifications

# AI ANALYSIS PIPELINE

## Step 1 – Image Preprocessing

Perform:

* Resize
* Normalize
* Noise Reduction

---

## Step 2 – Lesion Segmentation

Use U-Net.

Requirements:

* Cover entire lesion area
* Accurate lesion boundary detection
* Red transparent lesion mask
* Neon Cyan boundary
* Full lesion coverage
* Smooth contour detection

Metrics:

* Dice Score ≥95%
* IoU ≥90%
* Pixel Accuracy ≥95%

---

## Step 3 – Classification

Use EfficientNet-B4.

Predict:

* Melanoma
* Basal Cell Carcinoma
* Benign Keratosis
* Nevus

---

## Step 4 – Heatmap Generation

Generate Grad-CAM heatmap.

Highlight suspicious regions influencing prediction.

---

## Step 5 – Analysis Metrics

Calculate:

* Confidence Score
* Risk Level
* Lesion Area %
* Border Irregularity
* Color Variation
* Asymmetry Score
